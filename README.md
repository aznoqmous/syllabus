# aznoqmous/syllabus

![Title](Assets/Sprites/broadside-logo.png)

Embark on a 5 minutes journey on your ship where you will collect coins to upgrade your boat and face the Pirate Boss !

This game was made in 4 days for ENJMIN 2025 Unity Workshop

![Gameplay](gameplay.gif)

## Controls
__Move :__ WASD/ZQSD

__Fire cannon :__ Left Click

__Open Shop :__ Keyboard <E> when standing near the shop
